About Us

At Le Mervetty you can choose from a variety of pastries, like mini pistachio cakes, Earl Grey Tea macarons and fluffy Pavlovas. Another option to try on the menu are the handcrafted milkshakes creatively made and adorned with toppings such as colorful sprinkles or rich brownie bars.

Le Mervetty, the original Merveilleux concept introduced to Beverly Hills in 2012. A French Patisserie, serving Merveilleux, Macarons & Milkshakes
