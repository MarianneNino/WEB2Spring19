Homeless jumps 12% in L.A. County

Published: June 3rd, 2019

the Homeless Count results are in and homelessness has risen by 12 percent in Los Angeles County (and 16 percent in LA City) in 2019. While "more than 27,000 people have been permanently housed since July 2017 with Measure H and other public funds and temporary shelter has been provided for thousands of others," these results indicate that residents are having a hard time remaining housed. We see this reflected in the sky-high need at our member food pantries among people who struggle to save up enough for rent & food. More
